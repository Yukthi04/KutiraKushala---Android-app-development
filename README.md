Kutira-Kushala – Micro-Factory Showcase Platform

Kutira-Kushala is a beginner-friendly Android application developed using Kotlin in Android Studio as part of the MindMatrix Internship Project. The application is designed to support rural cottage industries and self-employment businesses by providing a digital platform where small-scale entrepreneurs can showcase their products, business details, and production capacity.
The main purpose of this project is to help home-based businesses such as basket weaving, agarbatti making, papad making, handicrafts, and food production gain better visibility and connect directly with bulk buyers. Many cottage industries struggle to reach larger markets due to lack of digital presence, and this app aims to solve that problem through a simple and accessible mobile application.

Project Objective

The objective of Kutira-Kushala is to digitally empower small cottage industries and promote self-employment opportunities by creating a platform that improves product visibility, encourages rural entrepreneurship, and supports self-reliance (Atmanirbhar Bharat).
